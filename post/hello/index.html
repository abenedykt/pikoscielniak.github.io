Welcome blog world

It’s quite common to have New Year’s Resolutions and I’m not the only one doing this. One of my New Year’s resolutions is to improve my English writing skills. The second one is starting my own blog. So, I decided to combine these two things together. My main goal for having my own blog is to create a base of my own knowledge and make it publicly accessible. Up to now, I had my notes in Evernote and I used this tool to store my thoughts. The majority of my notes are private. I am afraid to make them public because of the quality of my writing, I’m not confident that they are well written. There are a lot of shortcuts in them, the English is poor, and the quality of the text is nonexistent. I believe that making my notes public on my blog will force me to improve them. I will see how that turns out in the near future.

My main point of interest is mainly programming. I work as C# ASP .NET and JavaScript developer. Currently I’m also playing with Angular 2, TypeScript and with Go. I’m also interested in TDD, DDD and Agile. Roughly speaking, I am keen on how to write good, maintainable code which does what it should do and fulfills the customer’s needs. Most of my posts will be related to these topics.

However, I want to have this blog not only as my personal knowledge base but also as an area where I can express my thoughts about various topics and things which have happened to me. My initial goal is to write and publish one post per month but I will see, maybe it will be more. I hope that this blog will bring joy not only for my but also for other people. So, welcome my blog world.

Piotr Kościelniak
